MF - MAURITIUS WITH SINGAPORE
7 Days 6 Nights

Visit 2 Countries 7 Major Cities
Mauritius: Port Louis 2N, Ile Aux Cerfs, Trou Aux Cerfs, Curepipe, Grand Bassin, Chamarel, Singapore: Singapore 3N, Overnight flight: 1N

Mauritius
Day 01 : Mauritius - Port Louis
Arrive at Mauritius. On arrival visit Pamplemousses - the Royal Botanical Gardens. Take a panoramic view of Port Louis city from Citadel Fort. The orientation tour of Port Louis includes Catholic Church, Mosque, High Court building, Port Louis Theatre, French Colonial buildings etc. Enjoy shopping at Caudan water front.
Day 02 : IIe Aux Cerfs Island, Sega Dance
Visit to Ile Aux Cerfs Island by high speed boat. Take a thrilling experience of parasailing. Enjoy swimming in the crystal clear blue waters of Mauritius. In the evening enjoy Sega Dance.
Day 03 : South Tour - Singapore
Visit the Floreal Square, Model Ship Factory, Trou Aux Cerfs - the Volcanic Crater, Curepipe - the scenic residential town, Garden Village, Grand Bassin-The Sacred Lake, Shiva Temple, Black River Gorge, Chamarel Waterfall and Chamarel - Seven coloured earth. Later board the flight to Singapore.
Singapore
Day 04 : Singapore - City Tour
On arrival City tour includes Orchid Botanical Garden, Singapore river boat ride, the statue of Sir Thomas Stamford Raffles, the Parliament House, Suntec City, Fountain of Wealth, City Hall, Orchard Road and Swiss-the tallest hotel in Singapore. Evening free for relaxation.
Day 05 : Singapore - Jurong Bird Park, Sentosa Island
Visit Jurong Bird Park. Take a Panorail ride, See the Talking Birds, Penguin Parade, All Stars Birds Show and stroll along the rustic suspension bridge that offers a breath-taking view of the waterfall and Aviary. Encounter the highest man-made waterfall. Experience the Cable Car Ride from Mt. Faber to Sentosa Island. Visit Images of Singapore - The Pioneers of Singapore, The Surrender Chambers, Festivals of Singapore and wax works. See Underwater World and admire the rare species of the marine world. Stroll down the Merlion Walk - a 120-metre-long Gaudi-inspired mosaic walkway. Take a thrill of Sky Tower or Luge Ride with Sky Ride, an unique experience. Enjoy the 'Songs of the Sea Show' in the evening. Take a mono-rail ride from Sentosa to Vivo City.
Day 06 : Singapore - Singapore Flyer ride, Shopping, Night Safari
Experience the Singapore Flyer ride offering the breathtaking panorama of the shimmering Marina Bay waterfront and historical landmarks dotting the city skyline. Take a MRT-Underground Train Ride. Free time for shopping in the evening. Visit Night Safari in the evening, take a Tram Ride, see Creatures of Night Show and enjoy dinner in the unique environment of Night Safari.
Day 07 : Departure from Singapore
Board the flight for your hometown. Tour Concludes.

D1 - DUBAI DELIGHTS
6 Days 5 Hotel Nights

Visit the 3 Major cities of UAE
UAE: Dubai 5N, Sharjah, Ajman.

UAE
Day 01 : Dubai
Arrive at Dubai in the evening.
Day 02 : Dubai - City Tour, Gold Souk Market
City orientation tour includes Jumeirah Mosque, Burj Al Arab photo stop - unique hotel of the world, Dubai Race Course, Sheikh Zayed Road, Golf Club, Dubai Museum from inside and photo stop at Burj Khalifa. Enjoy a drive through Palm Islands and shopping at Gold Souk Market.
Day 03 : Dubai Shopping Mall - Dhow Cruise
Visit The Dubai Mall's Aquarium, officially earned the Guinness World Record for the world's "Largest Acrylic Panel". Free time for shopping at The Dubai Mall. Evening enjoy the Dhow Dinner Cruise - a traditional wooden sailing vessel which sails from the Heritage village. Relax and experience the beautiful atmosphere of the city by night.
Day 04 : Dubai - Deira City Centre, Desert Dune Safari
Free time for shopping at Deira City Centre shopping mall in the morning. Enjoy the most popular Desert Dunes Safari by a Four Wheel drive land cruiser and the exciting Belly Dance show with dinner.
Day 05 : Dubai - Mall of the Emirates, Snow Park
Visit Mall of the Emirates - the ultimate entertainment and shopping resort. Enjoy snow slides and photography in Snow Park.
Day 06 : Sharjah, Ajman - Departure from Dubai
Excursion to Sharjah. Journey starts at the Cultural Square, then continues to Ajman's enchanting museum, housed in an ancient fort. Pass by the majestic King Faisal Mosque, the Pearl Monument and the fabulous Blue Souk, where more than 600 shops sell carpets, gold, silver, antiques and many other goods. Later we take an orientation City tour of Ajman. In the evening arrive at Dubai airport to board the flight for your hometown. Tour Concludes.

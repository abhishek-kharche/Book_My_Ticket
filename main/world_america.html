U1 - AMAZING AMERICA
16 Days 15 Hotel Nights

Visit 7 Major Cities of United States
America: Washington 1N, Niagara Falls 2N, New York 2N, Orlando 4N, Las Vegas 2N, Los Angeles 2N, San Francisco 2N
Day 01 : Washington
Arrive at Washington. On arrival visit to Washington Monument, Lincoln Memorial followed by the orientation city tour which includes the Union Railway Station, Capitol Hill and Pentagon building. Visit the Smithsonian Air and Space Museum. Photograph yourself at the background of White House.
Day 02 : Washington - Hershey's Chocolate world - Niagara
Proceed to Niagara. En-route visit Hershey's chocolate world, visitors will have the opportunity to learn how chocolate is made and even try the delicious food and desserts. Arrive at Niagara in the evening. Free time to stroll around Niagara Falls - the greatest natural wonder of the world.
Day 03 : Niagara Falls
View the Niagara Falls, visit Goat Island, experience the thrill of Cave of the Winds, board 'Maid of the Mist' cruise to take a close encounter with the falls. Explore the Niagara falls illuminations in the evening.
Day 04 : Niagara - Corning Museum of Glass - New York
Proceed to New York. En-route visit the Corning Museum of Glass, which includes Glass Gallery - contains one of the best collections of glass art and The Hot Glass Show - a glassblowing demonstration. Enjoy shopping at The Glass Market, which offers a wide range of glass art. Arrive at New York in the evening.
Day 05 : New York - City Tour - Liberty Island
Enjoy magnificent view of the New York city from the 86th floor observatory deck of Empire State Building. Travel by ferry to visit Statue of Liberty on Liberty island. Panoramic city tour includes United Nations Building, Rockefeller Center and 5th Avenue, pass by the Central Park and Ground Zero Point - the site of the World Trade Center. Free time for shopping. Enjoy an illuminous drive along the famous Times Square and the Manhattan city by night.
Day 06 : New York - Orlando
Flight to Orlando. Arrive at Orlando - America's premiere vacation destination.
Day 07 : Orlando - NASA Space Centre
Explore NASA - Kennedy Space Centre Launch Headquarters, unique chance to visit NASA's launch and landing facilities. Experience interactive simulators, live shows and jaw-dropping encounters with massive rockets, as well as have the opportunity to meet a real member of NASA's Astronaut Corps.
Day 08 : Orlando - Epcot Centre
Day of excitement at Epcot Centre. Enjoy Space Ship Earth, Wonders of Life, Universe of Energy, Test Track, Journey into the future world, Tapestry of Dreams Parade in World Showcase and spectacular fire works.
Day 09 : Orlando - Disney World - Magic Kingdom
Full day fun at Disney World. Enjoy various rides like Space Mountain, Big Thunder Mountain, Splash Mountain, Tomorrow Land, Haunted Mansion, Spectacular shows, Animated Classics and Shake hands with your favourite Cartoon Characters like Mickey Mouse, Minnie, Pooh, Donald Duck, Tigger, etc. Enjoy Disney Parade in the evening.
Day 10 : Orlando - Las Vegas
Flight to Las Vegas. On arrival at Las Vegas stroll around the Famous Las Vegas Strip. In the evening enjoy Casino in various hotels, walk down the Las Vegas Strip and visit some of the best hotels known for their interiors like Luxor, MGM, Venetian, Bellagio etc.
Day 11 : Las Vegas - Grand Canyon
Early morning depart to Grand Canyon by small plane to enjoy the aerial view of the famous Canyons and Colorado river. In the evening once again see various hotels known for Casinos and shows like - The Musical fountain show at Bellagio. The Volcanoat Mirage etc. Enjoy shopping at the forum shops. Enjoy entertainment show the evening.
Day 12 : Las Vegas - Los Angeles
Arrive at Los Angeles - the world famous city of Hollywood stars. Drive through the rich and famous Rodeo Drive, view Beverly Hills Area, Hollywood Drive and Sunset Boulevard. Visit Madame Tussuad's Wax Museum.
Day 13 :                Los Angeles - Universal Studios
Full day excitement at the Universal Studios. Day of excitement begins with a "behind-the-scene" tour of Hollywood's busiest back lot, see how and where movies and TV shows are filmed. Face King Kong as he unleashes 30 feet and 30,000 pounds of howling, growling, living terror-just 36 inches from your unbelieving eyes! Be thrilled by the "Stunt Show Cowboys"; Relive the attack by the shark from "jaws", experience an earthquake, or watch the mesmerizing "Water World" show, feel the flames of"Backdraft" and the newest attraction "Jurassic Park"! Universal Studios, where Hollywood comes to life!
Day 14 :                Los Angles - San Francisco
Proceed to San Francisco. Drive through Malibu coastal via Zuma beach. Free time for shopping at Walmart. Arrive at San Francisco in the evening.
Day 15 :                San Francisco, City tour - Sausalito City
City tour includes City Hall and China town. Visit Twin Peaks, Golden Gate Park and the Golden Gate Bridge. Visit the Union Square, enjoy the cable tram ride to Lombard street followed by a short walk on the Crooked Street and Fisherman's Wharf. Enjoy SFO Golden Gate Bay Cruise. Visit Sausalito city, situated near the northern end of the Golden Gate Bridge. Developed rapidly as a shipbuilding center in World War II, is a picturesque residential community, and as a tourist destination.
Day 16 :                Departure from San Francisco
Board the flight for your home town. Tour Concludes.

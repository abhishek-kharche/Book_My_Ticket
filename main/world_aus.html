A1 - AUSTRALIA NEW ZEALAND
20 Days 19 Hotel Nights

Visit 2 Countries and 12 Major Cities
New Zealand: Christchurch 2N, Wanaka, Queenstown 3N, Rotorua 1N, Auckland 2N. Australia: Sydney 3N, Canberra, Cairns 2N, Brisbane, Gold Coast 3N, Melbourne 3N, Philip Island

New Zealand
Day 01 : Christchurch
Arrive at Christchurch. On arrival free time for relaxation.
Day 02 : Christchurch, City tour
Visit Christchurch Museum from inside, Antarctic center and South Pole Attractions. City tour includes Botanical Gardens along the Avon River and Cathedral Square. Later enjoy the memorable Tram Ride within the city.
Day 03 : Christchurch - Wanaka - Queenstown
Proceed to Queenstown. En-route visit Lake Tekapo - carved out of mountain rock by glaciers and picturesque Lake Pukaki. We may have a Glimpse of Mt. Cook (subject to weather conditions). Visit Puzzling world at Wannaka. On arrival at Queenstown free time for relaxation.
Day 04 : Queenstown, City Tour
Visit the Arrowtown museum from inside. See the Bungy Jumping Point. Experience the unique Jet Boat Adventure in Kawarau river. Enjoy Gondola ride to Bob Peak. Evening free to stroll around Queenstown's beautiful waterfront and enjoy shopping.
Day 05 : Queenstown - Milford Sound
Full day excursion to Milford Sound. See the Wakatipu Lake, Te Anau Lake, and Chasm water fall as you experience the most scenic travel through the Fjiord Land National Park by Milford Sound Cruise.
Day 06 : Queenstown - Auckland - Rotorua - Waitomo
Flight to Auckland. On arrival proceed to Rotorua. En-route visit Waitomo caves and see the Glow Worm Grotto with a Silent water boat ride (subject to operation) and Government garden. Enjoy Maori Dance Concert in the evening.
Day 07 : Rotorua - Auckland
Visit the famous Whakarewarewa Thermal Reserve, Agro Dome Sheep shearing show. Arrive at Auckland.
Day 08 : Auckland - City Tour
City tour includes Mt. Eden, Harbour Bridge, Queens Street and Waterfront, Auckland Sky Tower Observatory Deck and Kelly Tarlton Underwater World. Free time for shopping in the evening.
Australia
Day 09 : Auckland - Sydney, GAP, Showboat Cruise
Flight to Sydney. On arrival panoramic tour of Bondi Beach and the GAP. In the evening board the Show Boat Cruise for dinner and dance while enjoying the spectacular view of Sydney harbor bridge and City Skyline.
Day 10 : Sydney - Canberra
Full day excursion tour to Canberra. On arrival at Canberra city tour includes Embassy Road, New Parliament House, Mt. Ainslie, War Memorial and National Capital Exhibition Center.
Day 11 : Sydney - City Tour, Shopping
Take a guided tour of Opera House from inside followed by a panoramic city tour which includes Harbor Bridge, Macquarie's Point and King's Cross. Enjoy magnificent views from Sydney Tower Observatory deck and the Virtual Sky Tour. Visit to the Darling Harbor. Free time for shopping at Darling Harbor.
Day 12 : Sydney - Cairns
Flight to Cairns. Visit the Butterfly Park and experience Army Duck Tour. Free time for shopping in the night market.
Day 13 :                Cairns - Great Barrier Reef - Cairns
Visit the natural wonder of the world - The Great Barrier Reef in a day adventure tour, enjoy glass bottom boat and Mini Submersible.
Day 14 :                Cairns - Brisbane City tour - Gold Coast
Enjoy the Tjapukai Dance show and try your hand at Boomerang Throwing. Enjoy Sky-Rail cable car ride through tropical wonderlands and Kuranda Rain Forest. Flight to Brisbane. On arrival orientation tour of Brisbane includes Town Hall, Kangaroo Point and South bank waterfront. Proceed to Gold Coast. Free time for relaxation.
Day 15 :                Gold Coast - Movie World, Conrad Jupiter Show
Visit Warner Brother's Movie World, enjoy Looney tunes musical show, Police Academy - Stunt show, Batman adventure, 4D show, Superman ride, Bat wing ride etc. All Star Parade, meet your favorite cartoon characters like Bugs Bunny, Tweety and Daffy. Take Fun rides like Lethal weapon, Scooby Doo and Spooky Coaster. In the evening enjoy Conrad Jupiter Show.
Day 16 :                Gold Coast - Currumbin - Sea World
Visit the famous Currumbin Bird Sanctuary. See Kangaroos, Koalas and hand feed the Lorikeet Birds. Enjoy various shows at Sea World like Ski Challenge, Sea Lion, Live Sharks, Dolphin, Polar Bear, Sea world eye ride and 4D Planet show. Take a Cable car ride and Monorail ride.
Day 17 :                Gold Coast - Brisbane - Melbourne
Flight to Melbourne. On arrival free time for relaxation and shopping.
Day 18 :                Melbourne - Phillip Island - Melbourne
Proceed to Philip Island. En-route visit Shrine of remembrance. Visit the Nobby Point to see several sea lions flopping about on rocks. Witness the spectacular world famous Penguin Parade in the evening at Phillip Island.
Day 19 :                Melbourne - Great Ocean Road, Helicopter Ride - Melbourne
Experience one of the world's finest coastal drives at Great Ocean Road, see the famous 12 Apostles rock formations including the Lock Ard Gorge. Enjoy a scenic helicopter ride. Later drive through Port Campbell National Park.
Day 20 :                Melbourne City tour - Departure from Melbourne
Visit MCG ground from inside, followed by St. Patrick cathedral, Captain Cooks cottage from inside and Fitzroy garden. Later orientation tour includes Town hall and Old Parliament house. Enjoy the city tram ride. Free time for shopping. Visit the world famous Casino in the evening. Board the flight for your hometown. Tour Concludes.
